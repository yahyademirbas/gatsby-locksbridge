# Kandemir Basmacıoğlu | Piyano

> “Kandemir Basmacıoğlu's remarkable musicianship and especially his keyboard dominance, which I had the chance to listen to, is impressive. His ability to combine his own ideas with those of the composer makes him a great pianist. I have no doubts about his future." –Cyprien Katsaris

Kandemir Basmacıoğlu, with a passionate combination of musicality and intellectual concern, reinvents music with the audience in every performance.

Basmacıoğlu started his piano career in Kyrgyzstan at an early age. Basmacıoğlu who worked with valuable teachers such as Anna Garibian and Gülnara Aziz during his education, had chance of working together with the names who are accepted as an authority in their areas; Michael Markov, Michael Roll, Petrushansky Boris Konrad Elser, Oxana Yablonskaya. As well as Turkey, Germany, Italy, Ukraine and Bulgaria he played around the world.

He won the İş Sanat Youth Talents auditions and gave a recital at İş Sanat within the scope of "A Weekend with Shining Stars".

In 2006, he joined the Cyprien Katsaris Piano Academy held in Germany. Cyprien Katsaris expressed Basmacıoğlu's thoughts on F. Liszt B minor Sonata performance with the following words: “Kandemir Basmacıoğlu, whose excellent performance I had the chance to listen to, is impressive with his remarkable musicianship and especially his keyboard dominance. His ability to combine his own ideas with those of the composer makes him a great pianist. I have no doubts about his future. "

In Germany's Thüringische Landeszeitung newspaper, the following words were made about Basmacıoğlu's performance, which had a wide repercussion in the press: "Kandemir Basmacıoğlu, with his self-confidence, promised a very bright future, while his fingers flying over the keys were moving between strong and soft melodies."

After he was honored with the DAAD Artist Scholarship awarded by German Government to continue his higher education in Germany, he won the Borusan Music Scholarship provided by Turkey's leading private art institution Borusan Art.

Basmacıoğlu also gave a recital at the 15th International Antalya Piano Festival. In February 2020, he was one of the five pianists who took part in the concert held at the Berlin Philharmonic Chamber Music Hall to mark Beethoven's 250th birthday.

After completing his studies with Vassily Lobanov at Hochschule für Musik Köln, he completed his Proficiency in Art studies at Anadolu University State Conservatory.

Since 2010, Kandemir Basmacıoğlu has been continuing his academic studies in the Piano Department of the Istanbul Technical University State Conservatory as Associate Professor

Pianist Kandemir Basmacıoğlu is definitely distinguished by his own generation with his original vision, his competence in the piano that made possible a very wide and distinguished repertoire, and the versatility of his musical perspective!
