# Golden Horn Brass

> "The quintet, which has been the source of motivation for composers for years..." – NTV

Turkey's first brass quintet. Golden Horn Brass, with its Romanian and Turkish artists, has been known world-wide. Blending ambition and virtuosity, Golden Horn Brass brings the audience to a unique musical adventure with their energy. While being one of the most vibrant chamber music groups, The Quintet is one-of-a-kind with its talented members.

Golden Horn Brass, which has the power of a full symphony orchestra in itself, almost knits the music like a golden fabric with its most delicate, fragile and crystal harmonies thanks to their members’ virtuosity. Enriching their programs by commissioning works of many composers, Golden Horn Brass has already caught the difference and the new.

The group members are also academicians which reflects to their repertoire choices and concerts they carefully curate for the young people. Carrying music wherever they go is a reason for their existence. Thanks to the productive and innovative structure of Golden Horn Brass, a member of LB Artists, they offer anything that is novel, providing an unforgettable experience.

Sophisticated arrangements of folk music tunes makes them the most entertaining of the concert series and festivals world-wide. They were also awarded the “Chamber Music Ensemble of the Year” at the Donizetti Classical Music Awards. Golden Horn Brass has an international audience and they performed in many important concert halls in Lithuania, Switzerland, Moldova, Germany and Azerbaijan.
